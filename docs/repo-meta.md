## First release notes (v1.0.0)

**Title:** `v1.0.0 — Are you really happy?`

```markdown
The first public release of Know Yourself OS — a private, local-first self-discovery journal in a single HTML file.

Highlights
- 🧩 10-pillar onboarding Profile (bilingual EN / 中文) you fill in once
- ⭐ AI-generated Mission / North Star from your own answers
- 🌅 Daily dashboard: intention, energy/mood/alignment pulse, streaks, memory call-backs
- 🗓️ Weekly plan → export to PDF or send to WhatsApp
- 🔄 Weekly review that compounds into next week
- 📓 Private journal whose happy memories resurface when you need them
- 🌿 Reset: a bad-day rescue built from your own words

Privacy
Everything is stored locally in your browser. No account, no server, no tracking.
AI generation is optional and bring-your-own-key; if you skip it, nothing ever leaves your device.

Get started: download index.html and open it. That's the whole app.
```
